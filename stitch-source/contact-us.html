Common Questions

Quick answers to help you navigate your visit.

local_parking

Where do I park?

Free parking is available in the Multi-Storey car park located at the North entrance. Disabled bays are situated directly in front of the main lobby.

groups

Visiting hours?

Most wards are open to visitors from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Specific units like ICU have restricted times—please check with the nurse station.

description

Registration docs?

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, and any recent medical referrals. New patients are advised to arrive 15 minutes early.
